Project Highlights

GREAT LOCATION

Centrally located in the heart of the Central Business District.

GOLDEN 1 CENTER

Located one block away from the most ambitious redevelopment in the region - The Golden 1 Center (Sacramento Kings Arena), DOCO will add 300k sqft of new retail space, Kimpton Hotel (250 rooms), 64 luxury condos.

BUILDING DESCRIPTION

Generous parking allocation with 1,094-stalls, 5-level connected parking garage, valet service.
